## Support for the Development of Project-Based PhD Programs

The procedure **“Support for the Development of Project-Based PhD Programs”** is part of the **“Education” 2021–2027** program and is co-financed by the **European Social Fund Plus (ESF+)**.  

This is the first competitive selection procedure announced under the Education Program and falls within **Priority 3 – “Connecting Education with the Labor Market”**.

The following have been approved for funding under the procedure:

- **26 projects**
- **Total value:** €29.6 million
- **Partners:**  
  - 25 higher education institutions  
  - 4 research organizations  
  - Agricultural Academy  

It is expected that **over 450 PhD students** will receive support.

---

## Objective of the Procedure BG05SFPR001-3.004

The main objective of the procedure **BG05SFPR001-3.004 – “Support for the Development of Project-Based PhD Programs”** is to promote **innovative PhD education** through:

- Encouraging collaboration between universities and research organizations
- Achieving higher quality in teaching and research outcomes
- Supporting the development of an innovative economy with a focus on:
  - **Industry 5.0**
  - **Green transition**
  - **Blue growth**
  - Fields of **TSSS(The Smart Specialization Strategy)**
  - Regional labor market needs

## Project “Smart PhD Lab 5.0”

The project by **the University of Telecommunications and Posts (UTP)**  
**“Smart PhD Lab 5.0 – Project-Based PhD Programs for Intelligent Growth in Telecommunications, Artificial Intelligence, Automation, and Mechatronics”** has a total value of:

- **€609,000**

### Partners

The project is implemented by UTP in partnership with:  

- [**Mining and Geology University “St. Ivan Rilski” – Sofia**](https://mgu.bg/)  
- [**Technical University – Varna**](https://www1.tu-varna.bg/tu-varna/)

### Distribution of Project-Based PhDs

- **UTP:** 5  
- **MGU:** 2  
- **TU – Varna:** 2

### Focus and Results

The project focuses on creating and developing a **doctoral school**, designing and introducing **specialized courses**, and conducting **interdisciplinary and innovative PhD programs**.

The trainings are aligned with:  

- The modern, dynamic, and high-tech environment
- Regional labor market needs
- Priority areas in the planning regions

Establishing doctoral schools will contribute to:  

- Higher teaching quality
- Improved research outcomes
