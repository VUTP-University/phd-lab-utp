Theoretical Foundations of Communication Engineering is a fundamental scientific field that 
studies the principles, models, and methods for the analysis, design, and optimization of modern communication 
systems and networks. It focuses on the theoretical aspects of information transmission, 
processing, and reception across various communication media, including wired, wireless, and optical channels.
