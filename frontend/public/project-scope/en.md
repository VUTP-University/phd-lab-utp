## Project Scope

The activities outlined in the project aim to contribute to the vision of **TSSS(The Smart Specialization Strategy) 2021-2027** â€“ transforming Bulgaria into an innovative, intelligent, green, digital, and connected country through the interaction of research, innovation, and technology, as well as enhancing international and cross-sector collaboration.

The results of the project will be applied within the territory of the Republic of Bulgaria in the practice of the project's target groups. The scope of the included activities aligns with the goals of supporting and attracting young specialists in higher education institutions. The project implementation will contribute to:

- Improving the applicability of the labor market and the quality of higher education  
- Enhancing knowledge and developing skills for the transition to a green economy across all levels of higher education  

### Specific Goals of the UTP Project

- Establishment of a **doctoral school**  
- Development and delivery of **innovative specialized courses for PhD students**, aligned with the modern high-tech environment and labor market requirements  
- Support and attraction of **young specialists** to higher education institutions  
- Ensuring access to **quality education**  
- Development of **interdisciplinary doctoral theses** focused on sustainable development, the strategic goals of TSSS 2021-2027, regional priority areas, business needs, and modern conditions  
- Contribution to **Industry 5.0, the green transition, and sustainable development**  
- Guaranteeing **gender equality** and respecting the rights of people with disabilities  
- Development of **innovation capacity**  
- Enhancing **the capacity and skills of academic staff**
