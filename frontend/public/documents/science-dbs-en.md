## Subscription Databases

### Web of Science

<img src="https://vilniustech.lt/images/4110/205/10/10_1/clarivate%20analytics.jpg" alt="Web of Science" width="150" />

Database suitable for citation reports, impact factors of journals, conference abstracts, and patent information.
- [Access Web of Science](https://www.webofscience.com)
- [Operating instructions](https://clarivate.com/webofsciencegroup/support/wos/)

### Scopus

<img src="https://library.vgu.edu.vn/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/scopus-1.png" alt="Scopus" width="150" />

The largest abstract and citation database of peer-reviewed literature, covering scientific journals, books, and conference proceedings.
- [Access Scopus](https://www.scopus.com)
- [User guide](https://service.elsevier.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/14799/supporthub/scopus/)

## Free Resources

### Google Scholar

<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/28/Google_Scholar_logo.png" alt="Google Scholar" width="150" />

Free web search engine for scholarly literature across many disciplines and sources.
- [Access Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com)

---

### DOAJ - Directory of Open Access Journals

<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/06/DOAJ_logo.jpg" alt="DOAJ" width="150" />

Free online list of open access journals curated by the community.
- [Access DOAJ](https://doaj.org)

---

### arXiv

<img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/bc/ArXiv_logo_2022.svg/960px-ArXiv_logo_2022.svg.png" alt="arXiv" width="150" />
